Sleepless in __________ by Epik High

The album “sleepless in __________” was released on March 11 2019. The name of the album is left blank so that listeners can name it according to where they are listening.

It tackles the topic of sleeplessness and according to the main rapper of the group, what keeps us sleepless could be what is the most precious to us or what we are most afraid of. The message behind each song surrounds the different reasons as to why people feel sleepless.

My favorite song from the album: Eternal Sunshine

mono. by RM

mono. is RM’s second solo mixtape following his 2015 release. The cover art of the album incorporates the word ‘playlist’ rather than ‘mixtape’ because the tracks were produced to flow from one song to another.

"‘mono,’ can mean black and white. The word mono is great because it also can mean “alone” or “one”. When mono is stuck with another meaning, it is kind of the opposite of its stereotype. I think it’s the best title that can bring all the tracks together." -RM

My favorite song from the album: Seoul (prod. HONNE)

Modal Soul by Nujabes

Modal Soul is the second studio album by Japanese hip-hop artist Nujabes. His album features artists like Cise Starr and Akin (of CYNE), Terry Callier, Shing02, Substantial, Pase Rock, Apani B and Uyama Hiroto. Modal Soul fuses jazzy, smooth rhythms and hip hop. It was the final studio album released during Nujabes' lifetime.

Nujabes had been making music since 1996. He composed the soundtrack for the critically acclaimed anime series Samurai Champloo.

My favorite song from the album: sea of cloud

Episode1: Love by So!YoON!

Episode1: Love is Soyoon's second studio album. Her album features a variety of great arists and explores many themes surrounding the artist's many personas.

"Various desires, as well as love stories, are expressed and I wrote it with the intention of ‘finding myself.’ That’s why even though I didn’t mean for the theme to be all about love, I ended up discovering new personas of myself through the themes of love and desire. It was a process to discover my new self." - Soyoon

My favorite song from the album: Till the sun goes up

boy. by offonoff
